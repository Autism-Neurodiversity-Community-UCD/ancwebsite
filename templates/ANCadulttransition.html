Regional Centers

Including our local regional center, Alta California–provide many developmental disability supports in the state of California, including the paid internship program (see section on employment, below). Indeed, regional center supports can include housing and many other supports, if you can demonstrate that you need these supports –regional center supports are some of the most potentially extensive disability supports in existence. Autistic people without intellectual disabilities are sometimes able to obtainregional centersupports under the “fifth category” of service eligibility, but this requires proof of disability-related challenges going back to childhood, whether or not you had a formal autism diagnosis at the time.

The Student Disability Center

The SDC requires proof of autism diagnosis to verify that students are autistic. It can be expensive to obtain suitable proof if you do not already have it! Proof is also required by a number of other types of support agencies. Different organizations may have different proof requirements.
