Dictionary of Autism

Written by autistic people, with a number of relevant terms such as stimming, echolalia, etc.
Encyclopedia of Autism Spectrum Disorders

(edited by Fred Volkmar from Yale) Written from the perspective of researchers and clinicians, so there is an emphasis on terms that are used in those communities rather than among autistic adults.
